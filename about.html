senbazuru (the act of folding 1,000 paper cranes)

Design is inherently profound, much like the concept behind senbazuru. Real change doesn’t come from simply folding paper. Curious enough, why put in the effort to begin with?

Dare I say that’s already a given. Rather, the act of folding reaffirms what instills us with passion, leading us to believe that we have the power to manifest anything into existence. Cranes symbolize the power of our intentions through steadfast dedication.

Deep down, this is what drew me to design: manifesting impact. Revisiting this idea of continuous iteration made me fall in love with the craft again. Caring to design 1,000 paper cranes is a feat, but designing the 1,001th, 10,000th, 1,000,000th is a lifelong pursuit of fulfillment.
