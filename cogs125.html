    • Reflecting on the course...


    I appreciated this course for feeling like a design incubator instead of a typical course, where we can use these assignment prompts to explore wild ideas.


    It's was hard at first to articulate how I grew as a designer since we jumped from one topic to another. But I defintiely have strong takeaways. The most impactful design framework I learned from this course was thinking about UIs in terms of pixel real estate.


    With the first two assignments, I distinctly remember the moments when Prof. Klemmer dared to ask, what if you removed everything except for THIS? I love referencing this for my future work. I'm forced to rank each element and ask how much value it's providing the user to complete their goal.


    And with the design experiment project, I found his insights on data visualization and experiment outcomes to be quite valuable. It's all about storytelling in the end, and I will never take that for granted even if it's not UI design.
