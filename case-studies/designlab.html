01 - Background

The potential for problem-solving with AI tools

The recent rollout of chat-based AI tools have changed problem-solving and creative work for many different fields. Being able to gather insights and explore problem spaces just by typing a prompt into an LLM provides great value, letting users quickly develop their ideas.

Problem

Catching up to speed with prior work

From an exploratory story conducted earlier in the project timeline, we revisited two major limitations with LLMs:

❌

Not immediately usable

Responses tend to be wordy and may contain inaccuracies, placing more burden for users to carefully synthesize insights.

❌

Lacks user guidance

Novices lack knowledge with writing prompts, reflecting critically on responses, and extracting key insights.

Preview

Building on these insights, we explore an alternative approach for interacting with LLMs. Instead via chat, we looked at design templates, a well-established strategy for helping novices think, organize information, and reflect on their work.
