01 - Background

The Marketing Power of FoodTok



Needless to say, TikTok opens up a lot of doors for small businesses to thrive.


Due to the personal and authentic feel of the platform, public trust has become more important than ever for food recommendations. This is especially seen through food personalities like Keith Lee, as millions admire him for "keeping it real" in the food space.


Problem

But what about the home kitchens?


Business POV

TikTok doesn't necessarily make running a small business any easier, especially for home kitchens. With less staff, they usually have to wear multiple hats to run their business. For them, social media skills are a nice-to-have rather than a necessity.

Customer POV

And even though home businesses aren't a new wave on TikTok, the infrastructure simply isn't there to help customers discover vetted home kitchens on their own (whereas small food businesses have more established means of promoting).

Solution Preview

7Ate9, ready to dine!

The 7Ate9 app encourages friends to connect with small businesses and home kitchens they haven't tried before, lowering the barriers for customers to discover and for businesses to grow.

05 - Final Deliverables

Final Deliverables

With the foundations in place, we jumped straight into the high-fidelity prototypes due to the time constraints mentioned before. I was responsible for designing the customer view.

Customer View

Friend-driven reccomendations

7Ate9 recommends home kitchens based on what friends like and what the user has not tried yet. 7Ate9 balances novelty with familiarity, using gamification and friend referrals to incentivize people to try what's out there.

Business View

Full awareness when customers are ready to pickup orders

For home kitchens, managing delivery and inventory is a more manual process. 7Ate9 provides more efficient ways to keep track of delivery details to prevent getting lost in transaction.
