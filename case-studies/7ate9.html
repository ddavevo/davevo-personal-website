01 - Background

The Marketing Power of FoodTok



Needless to say, TikTok opens up many doors for small businesses to thrive.


Due to the personal and authentic feel of the platform, public trust has become more important than ever for food recommendations. This is especially seen through food personalities like Keith Lee, as millions admire him for "keeping it real" in the food space.


Problem

Virality is not the same as visibility


Business POV

TikTok doesn't necessarily make running a small business any easier, especially for home kitchens, often overlooked and left behind. With less staff, they usually have to wear multiple hats to run their business. For them, social media skills are a nice-to-have rather than a necessity.

Customer POV

And even though home businesses aren't a new wave on TikTok, the infrastructure simply isn't there to help customers discover vetted home kitchens on their own (whereas small food businesses have more established means of promoting).

Solution Preview

7Ate9, ready to dine!

The 7Ate9 app encourages friends to connect with small businesses and home kitchens they haven't tried before, lowering the barriers for customers to discover and for businesses to grow.
