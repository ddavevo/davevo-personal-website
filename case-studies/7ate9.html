01 - Background

The Marketing Power of FoodTok

As a marketing tool, TikTok offers small restaurants and home kitchens the opportunity to reach new audiences and attract more customers through sudden spikes in virality.


Due to the personal and authentic feel of the platform, public trust has become more important than ever for food recommendations. ‚ÄúOrdinary‚Äù voices can be quite powerful. We can see this through Keith Lee‚Äôs popularity, as millions are tuning in so that they can filter out the hidden gems from overrated and pricey places.

Virality is not the same as visibility

Since small businesses often have to wear multiple hats to run their kitchens, social media skills are a nice-to-have rather than a necessity. And so they may not have the tech-savvy or knowledge of current internet trends to promote themselves effectively.


Despite being exposed to more options, customers still find it hard to discover small businesses on their own since online feeds don't always show places locally.

Solution Overview

The 7Ate9 app encourages people to try new places with friends and connect with the chefs while easing business management and promotion for local food joints along the way.

02 - Research

Exploring the dynamic between social eating and trust

To dive deeper into what makes small businesses tick, I decided to perform competitive analysis and literature research to scope out how these businesses are catered to and how technology is used in the context of social eating.



Here's what we learned:


Localization

People feel a stronger sense of community through localized promotion because it provides familiarity and emotional connection.
